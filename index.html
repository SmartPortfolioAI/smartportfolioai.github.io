Nifty Consistent Compounder

Nifty Consistent Compounder is a delta neutral strategy which tries to capture the index in a range on a positional basis. The strategy aims to target a return of 2.5% to 3% on a monthly basis.

The strategy has delivered more than 30% returns including brokerage and slippage expenses for the year of 2023. The consistent performance of strategy can be seen in the Equity Curve.

Three words that define the philosophy of this strategy are Peace of Mind, Low Risk Consistent Performer (LRCP), Alternative Income.

Expiry Alpha Star


Expiry Alpha Star is a delta neutral strategy which tries to capture the index in a range on an intraday basis for index expiry. The strategy aims to target a return of 3% to 4% on a monthly basis.


The strategy has delivered around 50% returns including brokerage and slippage expenses for the year of 2023. Three words that define the philosophy of this strategy are Peace of Mind, Medium Risk Consistent Performer (MRCP), Alternative Income.
