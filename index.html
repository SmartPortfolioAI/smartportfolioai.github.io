Frequently Asked Questions

What is QSAlpha?
QSAlpha is a research membership portal providing up-to-date independently simulated out-of-sample backtests, with extensive performance statistics and implementation rules for the latest publicly available systematic trading strategies based on leading academic finance research.

How frequently are new strategies added?
We currently add strategies on a monthly basis after an extensive in-house research period.
Our strategies are thoroughly researched to ensure that they are simple to understand and provide good risk-adjusted and diversified performance. Hence for any strategy that we do include, we have not included many more.

How many strategies are implemented?
We currently have 10 buy & hold (static asset allocation) portfolios and 16 monthly dynamic tactical asset allocation strategies simulated, which are continually updated out-of-sample on a daily basis.
New strategies are added after a period of in-house research on a monthly basis.

Where do your strategy ideas come from?
Our strategy implementations are derived from trading rules outlined in publicly available academic sources such as research networks, financial journals and pre-print servers. We do not implement or backtest proprietary strategies from non-publicly available sources. We always provide detailed references to the original author's work for each strategy. In addition we attempt to provide references for strategies that are similar to those presented. We also provide a selection of our own custom strategy ideas that have featured on QuantStart.com in the past.

Do any of the strategies use margin or leverage?
Most of the presented strategies do not employ leverage and are long-only. However, for educational purposes we have provided alternative versions of some strategies where we have increased leverage. Such strategies are clearly marked. We also provide long/short portfolios for certain strategies, which inherently utilise margin in the form of borrowing stock from a simulated brokerage.

Are all of your provided strategies backtested?
Yes. All of the strategies we present are backtested out-of-sample up to the close of the previous trading day from the current day.

What tools do you use for simulated backtests?
All of the strategy backtests are simulated with the latest version of our own open source backtesting engine to further increase transparency of implementation.

Will you test my strategy rules?
While we do not test 'custom rules', we are open to suggestions to backtest strategies that are derived from publicly available research sources, such as research networks, journals or pre-print servers.

Can I pay with PayPal?
Yes. Our payment provider allows payment via credit cards and PayPal.
